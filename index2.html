REWIND OF THE UNIVERSE
Sci-Fi • Mythology • AI • Consciousness

Logline

When a forgotten technology from the 1760s resurfaces, humanity unlocks the ability to connect the human brain to the universe itself—triggering a war between souls, artificial intelligence, governments, and the creators of existence.

Part 1 – Universe

Souls exist after death in a parallel realm, silently influencing the living.

Part 2 – Brain

An ancient invention connects the human brain to the universe of souls.

Part 3 – Government

A hidden authority manipulates destiny through soul access.

Part 4 – AI vs Souls

Artificial intelligence battles souls for control of reality.

Part 5 – God

God intervenes and reveals the truth of creation.

Final Collapse

The universe contracts into silence. Time rewinds.
