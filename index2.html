REWIND OF THE UNIVERSE
Sci-Fi • Mythology • AI • Consciousness

Logline

When a forgotten technology from the 1760s resurfaces, humanity unlocks the ability to connect the human brain to the universe itself—triggering a war between souls, artificial intelligence, governments, and the creators of existence. As reality collapses, one man becomes the final link between God and a reborn universe.

Synopsis

“Rewind of the Universe” explores life after death, cosmic consciousness, divine creation, and hidden technology. What begins as scientific discovery evolves into a universal collapse involving AI, souls, God, and a single human destined to witness the end—and the rebirth—of existence itself.

Story Breakdown

Part 1 – Universe: Souls exist after death in a parallel realm influencing the living.
Part 2 – Brain: An ancient invention connects the human brain to the universe of souls.
Part 3 – Government: A hidden authority manipulates destiny through soul access.
Part 4 – Corporate: Artificial intelligence battles souls for control.
Part 5 – God: God intervenes, revealing the truth of creation.
Part 6 – Man: One human survives the collapse of all universes.
Part 7 – World: Earth, time, and reality dissolve.
Part 8 – New Universe: God creates a pure world without technology.
Part 9 – Rewind: Time rewinds—except for one man.
Part 10 – The End: A temple is built to preserve the truth of the past universe.
